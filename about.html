Company Profile

Gravitas Enterprises Pvt. Ltd., established in 1989, is engaged in manufacturing of small Reciprocating Small Air Compressors, Pressure Vessels, and Metal Structures.

We are also engaged in fabrication of MS & SS structures for conveyors, water treatment plants, ash handling units.

We manufacture vessels starting from 50ltrs capacity to upto 24M3 capacity for compressed air storage, and upto 100M3 capacity for DM / RO Water treatment plants applications.
