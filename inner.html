Company Profile


Gravitas Enterprises Pvt. Ltd., established in 1989, is engaged in manufacturing of small Reciprocating Small Air Compressors and Pressure Vessels.

 

We are also engaged in fabrication of MS & SS structures for conveyors, water treatment plants, ash handling units.

 

We manufacture vessels starting from 50ltrs capacity to upto 24M3 capacity for compressed air storage, and upto 100M3 capacity for DM / RO Water treatment plants applications.


Manufacturing units at two locations in Sector-59, & Sec-24 classified industrial estates, Faridabad :

- Plot No.60, Mother Unit & Accounts
- Plot No.167, Sec-24

 

Total ground area 40000 sq.ft.
Additional office space 1500 sq.ft.(1st floor)
