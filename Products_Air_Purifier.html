Air Purifier System

This unique two tower filtration system is an effective combat system to eliminate both oil and moisture / water.
Compressed, impure air, loaded with moisture and oil enters the Air Purifier system, wherein moisture and oil are separated out by way of surface impingement and capillary effects. The high grade activated carbon eliminates oil to the extent almost 99%. The moisture droplets separated are collected at the bottom in the polycarbonate bowl provided. For best result, this system has to be installed on the coolest / fartherest part of the compressed air-line. It may be noted this system cannot remove moisture and oil in the vapor form.

The air purifier is an independent unit, can be very simply installed along with the compressed air pipeline, after the air-compressor.

 

 

Notes :

  • Models available for Ex-Stock: AP-30, AP-50,for 30 cfm & 50 cfm respectively , for 15 kg/cm.2  (max.) working pressure.
