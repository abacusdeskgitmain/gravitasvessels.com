ACTIVITED CARBON FILTER (A.C.F)
The most commonly used air compressors have a lubricated compression chamber. The oil-content in the compressed air produced depends on the type of lubricated system (splash, force-feed, injection etc.), as well as, on the temperature of oil. For example, in oil injected screw compressor, an incorporated oil removal system would reduce the oil content to not below 20 to 30 mg per 100 cubic feet of compressed air. Further, upto 10 times more oil passes through filters at 40ºC then at 20 ºC of oil temperature. GRAVITAS activated carbon filter can cut the oil content to about 0.02 mg per 100 cubic feet of compressed air, oil temperature being at 20 ºC.

This carbon filter removes Oil to the extent of 99.9% (approx).
GRAVITAS ACFs are being used in various application in numerous industries. GRAVITAS A.C.F. uses special high grade activated carbon for effective oil removal and is a simple on line equipment.
The A.C.F. can be installed after the lubricated air compressor and fitted with the compressed air pipeline.
GRAVITAS ACFs are manufactured under strict quality systems, and are designed for high efficiency and durability by qualified, experienced personnel.

AIR PURIFIER SYSTEM
This unique two tower filtration system is an effective combat system to eliminate both oil and moisture / water.
Compressed, in pure air, loaded with moisture and oil enters the Air-Purifier through the oil-filter and leaves the system through the moisture separator. The output air is now oil-free and also moisture-free, which in turn would prevent any damage to the pneumatic applications. The efficiency of the moisture separation can be further enhanced if a water-cooler is installed before the moisture separator.
The Air purifier is an independent unit, can be very simply installed along with the compressed air pipeline, after the air-compressor.
